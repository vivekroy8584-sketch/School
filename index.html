Our Story

Shaping futures in Belur since 2013

Founded by Lt. Vyas Prasad Roy, Modern Academy stands as a beacon of quality general education in the Sapuipara community of Howrah.

  • 📚
    Academic Excellence
    Rigorous curriculum designed to develop critical thinking and deep subject mastery.
  • 🌱
    Holistic Development
    Sports, arts, scouts, and cultural events create well-rounded individuals.
  • 🤝
    Community Values
    Rooted in the Belur community, we foster respect, service, and togetherness.
